Research

All quantum systems are in principal ‘open’, that is, a quantum system is influenced in some way by its external environment. In many cases this induces complex behaviour that cannot be captured using traditional theoretical methods. In SOQS we develop novel tools and techniques capable of describing the behaviour of open quantum systems strongly coupled to their environment. Using these tools we investigate the implications that such interactions have on realistic quantum systems, with relevance to a broad range of fields, including: quantum technologies, quantum biology, and quantum thermodynamics.
